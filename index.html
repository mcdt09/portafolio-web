About me...

I was born in Edo. of Mexico on June 23, 1997.
Although at just 5 years old, my family and I moved to Playa del Carmen, Quintana Roo. And I have learned that I would not change that for anything in the world.

"Playense de vida, chilanga at heart"

I love animated series, comics and video games from which I always try to be inspired. Also a good lo-fi song always helps, although I will not deny my love for alternative rock, heavy and modern rock.
I believe in hard work and that nothing in life is guaranteed.
If you want something, you have to work hard to get it. I always seek to continue learning from everything I do and at the same time, whenever I can help those who may need it.

I come from a family of truly fighting people who do not allow themselves to be defeated easily, and they are always ready to get ahead with one foot to the finish line.
